The Sazi.life Family Story

Sazi.life is a heartfelt initiative by Salatiso, inspired by the enduring bonds of family and the shared joy of discovery. "Sazi" – "we know," "we understand" – reflects our belief that the strongest learning happens when generations connect, share wisdom, and explore the world together.

We are creating a warm, inviting space where every family member, from the youngest child to the wisest grandparent, can find something to ignite their curiosity and passion. Whether it's delving into isiXhosa, creating with code, or mastering home skills, Sazi.life is about growing together.
