Our Sazi Story

Mhlekazi Salatiso, a son of Transkei, draws inspiration from the resilient Aloe that thrives in the Eastern Cape landscape. This plant, much like the spirit of our people, endures and flourishes against the odds. Sazi.life is born from this spirit – a deep-seated belief in the power of accessible knowledge to nurture growth and self-sufficiency, especially for our youth and rural communities.

"Sazi" – meaning "we know" or "we understand" – is more than a name; it's our guiding principle. We aim to create a holistic educational ecosystem where learning about our Xhosa heritage, mastering modern technology through Sazi Code & Create, or acquiring practical skills via Sazi Home Life and Sazi Skills Hub, all contribute to a well-rounded, empowered individual.

We blend the rich oral traditions and community wisdom with modern, interactive learning methods. Sazi.life is a space for every generation to learn, create, and grow, fostering a community rooted in understanding and aspiring towards a brighter future.
