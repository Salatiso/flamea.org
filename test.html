About Sazi.life

Salatiso, Founder of Sazi.life

Sazi.life, founded by Salatiso, is rooted in a deep passion for making knowledge accessible to everyone, especially the youth and rural communities. "Sazi," an isiXhosa word meaning "we know" or "we understand," perfectly encapsulates our mission to foster learning, critical thinking, and self-sufficiency.

Growing up in Transkei and witnessing the power of community and informal wisdom alongside formal education, Salatiso was inspired to create a platform that blends the best of both worlds. Sazi.life is more than an educational website; it's a holistic ecosystem designed to empower individuals across generations with academic knowledge, practical life skills, and cultural understanding.

Our modules, from Sazi isiXhosa to Sazi Code & Create and Sazi Home Life, are crafted to be interactive, engaging, and adaptable to various learning styles and needs. We are committed to bridging the digital divide and providing tools that help individuals not just to learn, but to create, innovate, and build better futures for themselves and their communities. Join us on this journey of lifelong learning and empowerment.
