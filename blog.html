Personal Stories & Testimonials

This page is a space for fathers, families, and individuals to share their experiences with discrimination in family courts and related systems. By raising awareness, fostering community, and amplifying voices, we can drive meaningful change together.

My own journey began with The Homeschooling Father, where I documented my struggles and triumphs, which later led to books like Goliath’s Reckoning and Beyond Redress, among others. These stories are more than just personal—they are a testament to resilience and the fight for justice. I invite you to share your own experiences here, so we can stand together and make our voices heard.
