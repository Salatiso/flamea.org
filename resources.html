Resources

Access a range of tools, templates, and links to support fathers and families in navigating legal challenges and advocating for their rights.

Downloadable Templates

Below are some useful templates to help you prepare for legal proceedings and advocacy efforts:

  • 1. Bringing Matter to Children’s Court - Form 02 (J767) is used to bring a matter to the Children’s Court in terms of section 53 of the Children’s Act, 2005 (Act No. 38 of 2005).
  • 2. Simple Affidavit for Children’s Court - A sample affidavit to assert your parental rights.
  • 3. Simple Affidavit for Equality or Children’s Court - A sample affidavit to assert your parental rights.
  • 4. Complaint to the Magistrates Commission - Challenge unethical magistrate conduct.
  • 5. Complaint to the Legal Practice Council - Address legal practitioners facilitating rights deprivation.
  • 6. Criminal Complaint Affidavit to the NPA - Report potential parental abduction or kidnapping.
  • 7. Complaint to the Commission for Gender Equality - Highlight gender discrimination in court.
  • 8. Complaint to the Public Protector - Address systemic discrimination and High Court failures.
  • 9. Request to the South African Law Reform Commission - Advocate for legal reform.
  • 10. Equality Court Form 2 Guide - A completed example for filing a discrimination complaint.
